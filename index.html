The Meaning of Al-Aman

One name, many assurances.

Al-Aman carries many meanings, and each one speaks to the heart of legacy planning — peace, security, safety, and trust. It shares its root with amanah, the sacred trust placed in our care.

That is the promise behind our name: to hold your wishes faithfully, and to carry them safely to the people who matter most to you.

Two Ways to Plan

Wasiat and Hibah, simply put.

Two trusted instruments for Muslim families — each with its own purpose, each part of a thoughtful plan.

وصية
Your Islamic Will

Wasiat

A written declaration that takes effect after you pass, directing a portion of what you leave behind to the people and causes you choose.

هبة
A Gift in Your Lifetime

Hibah

A gift of ownership made while you are still here — given freely, with love, to those you wish to provide for.
